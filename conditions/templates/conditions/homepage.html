How We Calculate Snorkeling Conditions

Our forecasts combine multiple weather factors to determine the best snorkeling conditions:

  • • Wave Height: We look for calm conditions with waves under 0.3 meters
  • • Wind Speed: Light winds under 4.5 m/s ensure minimal surface disturbance
  • • Water Temperature: Comfortable temperatures between 22°C and 29°C
  • • Daylight Hours: Conditions are only rated during daylight for safety and visibility
